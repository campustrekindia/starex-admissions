Admissions Criteria

Eligibility at a Glance — Admissions 2026–27

Engineering — B.Tech Admission Gurugram

  • 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Maths (PCM)
  • Minimum 45% aggregate marks (40% for SC/ST)
  • JEE Mains score preferred (not mandatory)
  • Lateral entry available for Diploma holders

Management — MBA / BBA College Gurugram

  • MBA: Any graduation with min 50% marks
  • CAT / MAT / CMAT score considered
  • BBA: 10+2 in any stream with 45% marks
  • Executive MBA: 2+ years work experience

Law — LLB Admission Gurugram

  • 5-Yr Integrated: 10+2 with min 45% (any stream)
  • 3-Yr LLB: Graduation with min 45% marks
  • LLM: LLB degree with min 55% marks
  • CLAT / LSAT score advantageous

Pharmacy & Arts / Science Admission

  • B.Pharma: 10+2 with PCB/PCM, min 45%
  • D.Pharma: 10+2 with Science, min 45%
  • Arts / Science: 10+2 in relevant stream
  • Ph.D: PG with min 55% + entrance test
